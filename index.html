Research

Broadly, I work at the intersection of:

  • Text-to-Table & Information Extraction — Schema-guided extraction, proposition-based reasoning, and table generation from long-form text
  • Multimodal LLMs — Evaluation under distribution shift, robustness, and grounding
  • NL2SQL & Interactive Systems — Real-time question answering and visualization over dynamic data
I aim to build practical, interpretable systems that improve how we extract and reason over structured information from text and multimodal data.
