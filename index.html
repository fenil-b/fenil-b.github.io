Fenil Bardoliya

I’m Fenil Bardoliya, an MS graduate in Computer Science from Arizona State University. I work at the Complex Data Reasoning and Analysis Lab (CoRAL), advised by Dr. Vivek Gupta. Previously, I was a Computer Vision Research Aide at DREAMS LAB and interned at Samsung Semiconductor India Research. I received my B.E. in Computer Science from BITS Pilani.

My research focuses on natural language processing, text-to-table generation, and multimodal reasoning—building systems that turn unstructured text into interpretable, structured data and reason robustly across modalities.

If you’re interested in NLP, text-to-table, or multimodal evaluation, feel free to reach out. Outside of research, I enjoy traveling, swimming, tennis, basketball, and the occasional game of Dota 2.
