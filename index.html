About

I work on natural language processing, text-to-table generation, and multimodal reasoning at the Complex data Reasoning & Analysis Lab (CoRAL), advised by Dr. Vivek Gupta. I hold an MS in Computer Science from ASU. Previously I was a Computer Vision Research Aide at DREAMS LAB, interned at Samsung Semiconductor India Research, and received my B.E. in Computer Science from BITS Pilani.

I build systems that turn unstructured language into interpretable structured data and study robust evaluation across modalities. If you work on NLP, tables, or multimodal evaluation, feel free to write.

Outside research: travel, swimming, tennis, basketball, and Dota 2.

Research

Broadly, I work at the intersection of structured prediction from text, information extraction, and multimodal LLMs—with an eye toward NL2SQL and interactive tools over data.

  • Text-to-table — prose to tables and structured targets for analysis and downstream use.
  • Information extraction — entities, relations, and semi-structured outputs from documents.
  • Multimodal LLMs — vision–language reasoning, grounding, and evaluation.
  • NL2SQL & interactive systems — natural language interfaces to data and explanatory agents.
